FAQ

Answers before you ask.

Is color separation really free?

Most shops charge $30–50 per color, so a 6-color design costs you $180–300 just in setup. We separate your artwork for free — no per-color fees and no flat fee, up to 6 spot colors or full process. Order with us and your separations stay free forever. Not ready to print with us yet? Keep the print-ready files for a one-time $15, credited back in full when you place your first order. This is our biggest differentiator and the #1 reason print shops switch to us.

What file formats do you accept?

Vector preferred: AI, EPS, PDF, SVG. Raster accepted at 300 DPI minimum: PNG, JPG, TIFF, PSD. If your file isn't print-ready, our art team will fix it free for orders over $100, or for a flat $25 art-prep fee on smaller orders.

What's your turnaround time?

Standard: 5 business days from proof approval. Need it faster? Rush production is available on request — just ask. Free digital proof returned within 4 business hours of upload.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes. If your transfers arrive defective, mis-printed, or don't match your approved proof, we reprint or refund — your choice, no questions. Press-quality issues on your end (wrong temp, time, or pressure) aren't covered, but we'll send a free press instruction card with every order.

What's the minimum order?

100 pieces is our minimum order. Ordering 10,000+? Contact us and we'll set you up with custom volume pricing.

Can I reorder a previous job?

Yes — every order ships with a reorder code. Enter it on the quote page and your exact specs + artwork load instantly. Reorders skip the proof step and ship in 3 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

USA + Canada via USPS/UPS. International orders ($150+) ship DHL, customer pays duties. Email us first for quotes outside North America.
