FAQ

Answers before you ask.

Why is color separation flat $30 instead of per-color?

Most shops charge $30–50 per color, so a 6-color design costs you $180–300 just in setup. We absorb that cost across our larger order book and charge one flat $30 fee no matter how many colors — up to 6 spot colors or full process. This is our biggest differentiator and the #1 reason print shops switch to us.

What file formats do you accept?

Vector preferred: AI, EPS, PDF, SVG. Raster accepted at 300 DPI minimum: PNG, JPG, TIFF, PSD. If your file isn't print-ready, our art team will fix it free for orders over $100, or for a flat $25 art-prep fee on smaller orders.

What's the difference between plastisol, DTF, and screen-print transfers?

Plastisol uses screened plastisol ink on release paper — durable, classic feel, best for 1–6 spot colors. DTF (direct-to-film) prints full-color CMYK + white onto film, then powdered with adhesive — unlimited colors, soft hand, low MOQ. Screen-print transfers are true silkscreened layers offering the longest wash life — best for bulk uniform runs of 250+.

What's your turnaround time?

Standard: 5 business days from proof approval. Rush options: 72hr (+$20), 48hr (+$35), 24hr (+$45). Free digital proof returned within 4 business hours of upload.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes. If your transfers arrive defective, mis-printed, or don't match your approved proof, we reprint or refund — your choice, no questions. Press-quality issues on your end (wrong temp, time, or pressure) aren't covered, but we'll send a free press instruction card with every order.

What's the minimum order?

25 transfers for plastisol and screen-print. 1 linear foot for DTF gang sheets (~24" × 12" of artwork). Below 25 transfers, DTF is almost always your best bet.

Can I reorder a previous job?

Yes — every order ships with a reorder code. Enter it on the quote page and your exact specs + artwork load instantly. Reorders skip the proof step and ship in 3 business days.

Plastisol heat transfers vs. DTF vs. screen-print transfers

Plastisol heat transfers are the classic choice for 1–6 spot-color designs. They feel like a screen-printed shirt, last 40+ washes, and accept Pantone color matching. Best for bold logos, athletic wear, and branded merch in moderate runs (50–10,000+ units).

DTF gang sheets (direct-to-film) print full-color CMYK artwork plus white onto film, then powder it with hot-melt adhesive. DTF supports unlimited colors, photo-real artwork, gradients, and tiny detail. It presses to cotton, polyester, blends, nylon, and even canvas. Perfect when your design has more than 6 colors or your order quantity is under 50 units.

Screen-print transfers are true silkscreened ink layers on release paper. They produce the most durable, longest-lasting transfer available (50+ washes easily) and shine on bulk uniform runs of 250 or more. Order them now and press them six months from now — they store cleanly.
